Our story

Nibras The story and the beginning

Nibras was founded on our belief that true vocational education should be practical, accredited, and connected to actual labor market needs. For more than three years in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, the Academy has been working to provide specialized training programs that help individuals and institutions develop their skills and build strong and sustainable career paths.

At Nebras, we believe that knowledge alone is not enough, but must be translated into applicable skills and tangible results in the professional reality.

About us: Nibras is a specialized vocational education and training platform that provides accredited and targeted programs for individuals, entities and institutions. We cover a range of vital specialties including:

  • Political science
  • Economics sciences
  • Corporate governance
  • Cyber ​​security
  • Hotel management
  • Logistics services

Our vision

To be a reliable reference in the field of professional training and accreditation at the region level, by providing educational programs with global standards and real impact.

Our message

Providing high-quality vocational education and training that contributes to empowering individuals, raising the efficiency of human resources, and supporting institutions with effective training programs that keep pace with the requirements of the times.

Our values

  • Credibility: Commitment to reliable content.
  • Quality: Highest standards of education.
  • Impact: Focus on practical results.
  • Continuous development: Lifelong learning.
  • Flexibility: An experience that suits everyone.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our programs and accreditations

Are the Academy programs accredited?

Yes, all Nibras Academy programs are accredited by reliable international and local bodies specialized in professional training.

How can I register for a program?

You can register easily by choosing the appropriate program, clicking the "Add to Cart" button, and then proceeding to checkout.

Is there technical and professional support after graduation?

Certainly! At Nibras, we are keen to provide continuous guidance to our graduates to ensure they get the maximum benefit from the programs in the professional environment.

Is the learning in-person or remote?

We provide flexible tracks that include remote training (live and recorded) to suit your schedule, in addition to some in-person programs.
