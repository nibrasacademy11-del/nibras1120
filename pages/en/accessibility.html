Accessibility

Our commitment to providing a smooth and accessible educational experience for everyone

1. Our commitment to Accessibility

At Nibras, we believe that education is a right for everyone. We are committed to making our digital platform accessible and usable to as many people as possible, regardless of ability or technology used.

2. Standards followed

We are constantly working to improve the site's compliance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG). This includes providing alternative text for images, ensuring appropriate color contrast, and structuring pages in a way that supports screen readers.

3. Support and assistance

If you experience any difficulty using our website, or need assistance accessing any of the Programs and training materials, please contact us via the “Contact Us” page and the support team will assist you and provide suitable alternatives.

4. Continuous development

We periodically review the Accessibility rating on our platform, and we welcome your suggestions and feedback that help us improve the educational experience to be more inclusive.
